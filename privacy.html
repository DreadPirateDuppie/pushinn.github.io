Privacy Policy

LAST_UPDATED: 19th April 2026

1. The Data We Collect

As a geospatial social network, we collect specific data required to provide our services:

  • Geospatial Data: Precise GPS coordinates of "Skate Spots" you map or interact with.
  • Media Content: Trick videos and photos you upload to the platform.
  • Identity Data: Username, email, and profile stats (Triple-Score metrics).
  • Technical Data: Device identifiers, IP addresses, and hardware specs (for video compression optimization).

2. How We Use Your Data

  • Community Mapping: GPS data is used to populate the decentralized spot map.
  • Validation: Media content is used for the "Tribal Verification" and Peer-to-Peer battle system.
  • Leaderboards: Your stats (Player Score, Map Score) are publicly displayed to facilitate the "Sponsor-Heat Pipeline."

3. Data Sharing & Third Parties

  • Object Storage: Videos are routed to Cloudflare R2 for hosting.
  • Analytics: We use anonymized data to improve the "Waze for Skating" intelligence layer.
  • No Sale of Personal Data: We do not sell your personal identity data to third parties.

4. Your Rights (UK-GDPR)

Under the Data Protection Act 2018, you have the right to:

  • Request access to your data.
  • Request deletion of your account and associated media.
  • Restrict how your "Crew-Only" spots are shared.

5. "Crew-Only" Privacy

Pushinn implements a tiered privacy architecture. Spots marked as "Crew-Only" or "Invite-Only" are cryptographically restricted to specified user IDs and are not accessible via the public API or map.
