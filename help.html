1. Introduction to Pushinn Pro

Pushinn Pro is a specialized social layer for the urban skateboarding community. Our mission is to facilitate skill-based competition and spot discovery through decentralized community verification.

2. The Tribal Onboarding Protocol

[REVIEWER_NOTICE]: If you are reviewing the app, use the credentials provided in your review notes to bypass the 24-hour verification window.

To ensure authenticity, Pushinn uses a dual-pathway onboarding system. New users must either provide verified footage or use an Invite Token from an existing community member.

3. The Closed-Loop Economy

Pushinn utilizes a "Ranking/Clout" system based on virtual points. These points are strictly non-monetary and hold zero real-world value. They cannot be purchased, traded, or withdrawn for fiat currency, ensuring absolute compliance with global mobile marketplace regulations.

4. Safety & Spot Governance

Our "Locked Off" protocol allows users to report unsafe or inaccessible conditions in real-time. This user-driven safety layer prevents session accidents and community friction.

5. Privacy Architecture

Pushinn implements "Crew-Only" privacy logic. Sensitive geospatial data (secret spots) is encrypted and visible only to authorized crew members, protecting urban subculture from public exploitation.
